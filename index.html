Definisi & Epidemiologi

  • Definisi: Hiatus hernias occur when there is herniation of abdominal contents through the esophageal hiatus of the diaphragm into the thoracic cavity[cite: 8].
  • Epidemiologi: The prevalence of hiatus hernia increases with age[cite: 10].
  • Statistik: Estimates go from about 10% under age 40 to ~30-50% in people over 50-70 years, with a slight female predilection[cite: 10].

Klasifikasi Tipe Hernia (Types)

Some divide them into four types[cite: 35]:

  • Type 1: sliding hiatal hernia (~95%)[cite: 36].
  • Type 2: paraesophageal hiatal hernia with the gastro-esophageal junction in a normal position[cite: 37].
  • Type 3: mixed or compound type, paraesophageal hiatal hernia with displaced gastro-esophageal junction[cite: 38].
  • Type 4: mixed or compound type hiatal hernia with additional herniation of viscera[cite: 39].

Karakteristik CT (CT Features)

  • Herniated organs: visualization of the stomach fundus (sliding hernia) or larger portions of the stomach and other viscera (paraesophageal hernia) above the diaphragm[cite: 41].
  • Widened esophageal hiatus: the opening in the diaphragm where the esophagus passes is widened, with separation of the diaphragmatic crura >15mm, providing a clear passage for tissue[cite: 42].

Tatalaksana & Prognosis (Treatment and Prognosis)

  • Symptomatic hiatus hernias, especially types 2-4, should be managed surgically[cite: 44].
  • In asymptomatic individuals, especially those that are elderly or have significant comorbidities, there is increased evidence that conservative non-surgical management is preferred[cite: 45].

Komplikasi (Complications)

A hiatus hernia containing the stomach may result in a gastric volvulus, which in turn presents as intestinal obstruction and may result in ischemia/infarction, or very rarely a tension gastrothorax, causing respiratory failure and eventual cardiac arrest[cite: 47].

Diagnosis Banding (Differential Diagnosis)

  • Retrocardiac empyema [cite: 49]
  • Epiphrenic esophageal diverticulum [cite: 49]
